## Aesthetic

Brutalist dark-mode. Zero border-radius, no shadows, monospace-only (JetBrains Mono), OKLCh color tokens.

## Principles

1. **Clarity over aesthetics** — Every pixel serves comprehension
2. **Show the data, not the chrome** — Maximize information density
3. **Structure communicates** — Spatial relationships and typography over decoration
4. **Direct manipulation** — Inline editing, canvas interactions, keyboard shortcuts

## Colors

Dark-mode only. Electric violet brand accent (hue 260) against achromatic grayscale. OKLCh tokens.

## Typography

JetBrains Mono only (`@fontsource-variable/jetbrains-mono`).
